| WU-Telegram-409 | Telegram bot 409 Conflict — rapid-retry fix + singleton enforcement | ALTA | ✅ RISOLTA 22/05 (commit `0d09018`). Sintomo: cascata 409 "Conflict: terminated by other getUpdates request" ad alta frequenza (centinaia/minuto).
